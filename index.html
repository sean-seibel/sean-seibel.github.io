How to Play:

· Players take turns placing stones of their color in a square on the board, black goes first.

· You may not place a stone beyond 2 spaces orthogonally or diagonally from the previous move, unless no such move exists.

· Scoring = total number of 4-in-a-rows + total number of non-overlapping 3-in-a-rows for each color.

· White has a 1.5 point handicap for going second.
