How to Play: Players take turns placing stones of their color in a square on the board, black goes first Scoring = total number of 4-in-a-rows + total number of non-overlapping 3-in-a-rows for each color For instance, a 5-in-a-row not connected to anything else would count for 3 points, for 2 different 4-in-a-rows, and the single 3-in-a-row which can fit without overlaps. White has a 1.5 point handicap for going second.
